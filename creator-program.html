❓ FAQ
Do I need a large audience to apply? ▾
Not at all! We welcome creators at all stages. What matters most is content quality and passion for education.
How do I get paid? ▾
Payments are made via PayPal or direct bank transfer on your tier's schedule (monthly, bi-weekly, or weekly).
Can I still post on other platforms? ▾
Absolutely. There's no exclusivity requirement. Post your content anywhere — we only ask that OnePrep gets early access to new content.
What content formats are supported? ▾
Videos, articles/blog posts, SAT question sets, audio lessons, and study guides are all supported on the OnePrep platform.
